These scenes are named not after what they unlock, but what can be used to unlock them

Highjump -> a simple secret plaque placed high up
Fireblast -> a burnable secret placed near the ground (before fireball is available)
Lightning -> a simple secret somewhere only accessible through shocking some machine and activating it
Fireball -> a burnable secret placed high up
StikyLight -> a secret only visible to light
Wind -> a simple secret under the falling leaves
Snow -> Triggers the boss and the end of the game
